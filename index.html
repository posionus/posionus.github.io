Cool Projects I've Worked On

I love applying my Software Development and Data Science skills to cutting edge projects in business and academia. Below you can see a selection of some of my previous work.

Forecasting Sales

I applied SARIMA & GARCH statistical timeseries models to forecast sales for an industrial frangrances & flavours company. I was able to reduce monthly forecasting error by 25% over the company's previous approach. I created a Power BI dashboard to display a plot of the forecasts. I also built an interactive PowerApp that used the forecast to estimate lifetime holding costs and stockout risk for purchased items.

Drone Rescue Vision

I piloted a quadcopter drone with a mounted thermal IR camera to capture images of participating university students floating in water. Then I trained a Convolutional Neural Net in Python to recognize and localize humans in the images. The ultimate goal of the project is to use autonomous drones for search and rescue of people lost in bodies of water.

Evolution Simulation

I simulated the evolution of yeast in different continuous culture systems. The simulation is agent based, modeling genetic level mutations in individual organisms. It was originally written in MatLab and then ported to Java. My work on this simulation enabled the professor I was collaborating with to receive grant funding to continue the project. The video on the left shows a population of simulated yeast evolving to a higher environmental fitness.

Controlling Drone Swarms

I designed various command and control strategies for autonomous drone swarms. The strategies were developed in an agent-based modeling language called NetLogo. I first-authored an academic journal paper about my work and presented at an academic conference to a large audience. My paper was cited multiple times.
